What is Climate Change?

Climate change is caused by human activities that release greenhouse gases, leading to long-term changes in weather and temperatures on Earth. It poses significant challenges to ecosystems, human health, and the economy. Urgent action is needed to reduce emissions and adapt to the changes.



Impact So Far

  • Earth's surface temperature has risen by 1.1 degrees Celsius due to human activities
  • Climate change is causing rising sea levels, which threaten coastal areas and small island nations
  • Climate change leads to more severe weather events like heatwaves, droughts, hurricanes, cyclones, and wildfires.
  • Greenhouse gases absorbed by oceans lead to ocean warming, acidification, and affect marine ecosystems.
  • Climate change hits vulnerable populations like those in poverty, indigenous communities, and areas prone to extrem weather events the hardest.
